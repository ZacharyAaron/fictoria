Pricing
          We have conference rooms to accommodate groups of 10, 20, 50, 150 and 200 and rooms are booked by the day. The price includes tea and coffee. Full catering is available at additional charge. Accommodation includes breakfast. You are invited to dine at our five star restaurant, but the price is not included in the room charge. All rooms have en-suite bathrooms, remote-control colour television with satellite channels, clock radio, internet and power connections for laptops. Each room also has a selection of popular fiction and non-fiction books we hope you will enjoy. There is a fridge, coffee-maker and hairdryer in each room and an iron and ironing board can be made available on request. We are pleased to arrange discounts for pre-paid group bookings, for businesses and individuals. We ask for a non-refundable deposit when reservations are made. All major international credit cards accept.
